What is Computer Science?

Introduction

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. It involves both theoretical and practical aspects, including algorithms, data structures, software design, and hardware.

Fields in Computer Science

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science
  • Cybersecurity

Importance of Computer Science

Computer Science plays a crucial role in modern society, enabling advancements in various fields such as healthcare, finance, education, and entertainment.
